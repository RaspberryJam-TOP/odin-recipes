Ramen

Description

A quick and easy ramen dish

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen package
  • 3 tbsp white miso paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 egg
  • sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Cook instant ramen
  2. Whisk miso paste, butter, soy sauce, and vinegar. Pour over noodles, stir
  3. Add sliced egg, garnish with sliced green onions
